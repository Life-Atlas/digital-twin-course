About Nicolas

Nicolas Waern builds systems that understand reality. As CEO of Life Atlas and founder of WINNIIO AB (Gothenburg, Sweden), he leads development of the world's first sovereign biological digital twin platform — where physics-based simulation replaces statistical guessing in human health.

With 25+ years spanning pharma, manufacturing, energy, defense, construction, health, and education, Nicolas brings rare cross-domain depth to every stage of digital transformation. He currently runs 31 specialized AI agents in production — replacing traditional team functions with autonomous systems that perceive, plan, act, and reflect.

An EU Expert Evaluator (EX2026D1413450), published researcher (ORCID 0009-0001-4011-8201), and co-chair of industry working groups with Fortune 500 CTOs, Nicolas speaks at the precise intersection of what is technically real and what is commercially viable. His SMILE methodology has guided digital twin implementations across 7 industry verticals on 3 continents.

Based in Gothenburg, Sweden. Available worldwide in English and Swedish.
