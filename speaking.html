About the Speaker

The practitioner
behind the ideas.

Nicolas Waern has spent 25+ years at the intersection of physical systems and digital representation — building digital twins before the term existed, and orchestrating AI agent systems before the tooling caught up.

He created the S.M.I.L.E. methodology — a five-phase framework (Sense, Model, Integrate, Learn, Execute) now applied across 7 industry verticals. He is an Oxford Guest Lecturer, Digital Twin Consortium Co-Chair, and the CEO of Life Atlas, a sovereign life operating system that positions the biological digital twin as the crown jewel of personal intelligence.

Nicolas speaks at the intersection of technology, human sovereignty, and civilisational impact. His talks are dense with real cases, zero vendor promotion, and a point of view that challenges audiences to think three levels deeper than the conversation they arrived with.
